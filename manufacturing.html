Our Facility

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Facility in Umbergoan, Gujarat

Located in Umbergoan, Gujarat, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is the backbone of Spinnerz Globe's commitment to quality, innovation, and craftsmanship. Designed to support high-precision production, the facility is equipped with advanced machinery, including injection molding units that enable in-house production of shells and accessories — ensuring complete control over quality, consistency, and design.

With a robust infrastructure featuring over 40 molds in various sizes and configurations, we offer exceptional flexibility to cater to diverse customer needs, and scalability to efficiently handle both large-volume orders and bespoke, limited-edition projects.

Our operations are driven by a highly skilled and experienced team, trained to uphold rigorous standards at every stage — from raw material processing to final assembly. With a deep focus on precision engineering, quality assurance, and timely delivery, we ensure that every Spinnerz Globe product not only meets but exceeds industry expectations.

At Spinnerz Globe, manufacturing excellence is more than a goal — it's a promise we deliver with every piece of luggage we create.
